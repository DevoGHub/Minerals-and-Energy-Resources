Minerals of Arunanchal Pradesh

Coal

Coal mining in Arunachal Pradesh helps the people of the state in using the mineral for multiple tasks like cooking, generating electricity and also steel production, cement manufacturing and also used as a source of organic chemicals.

Petroleum and Natural Gas

There are many uses of Petroleum and natural gas like transportation which helps the people of the state travel easily. Another example of petroleum and natural gas is power generation.

Dolomite

Dolomite is an ornamental stone, a concrete aggerate, and a source of magnesium oxide. It is used to for marble building stones in Arunachal Pradesh and in manufacture of glass and ceramic glazes.

Limestone

It is a raw material for the manufacture of quicklime( calcium oxide), slaked lime( calcium hydroxide), cement and mortar. Pulverized limestone is used as a soil conditioner to neutralize acidic soils. It is added to toothpaste, paper, plastics, paint, tiles and other materials as both white pigment and a cheap filler.

Energy Resources in Arunanchal Pradesh

Solar Energy

The Arunachal Pradesh Energy Development Agency (APEDA) has issued a tender for developing off- grid solar photovoltic projects to be installed in the state. Through this tender, 46 off- grid solar systems of capacity 25KW each will be set up across the state, aggregating over 1.15 MW solar capacity.

Wind Energy

The objective of the programme on Small Wind Energy And Hybrid Systems is to develop technology and promote applications of water pumping windmills and Wind solar hybrid systems.
